About Me

I am a final year mechatronics engineering student who is passionate about electronics and telecommunication.
I am fascinated by how those tiny components come together to create a variety of powerful gadgets that make your life easier, and also at how it allows quick and efficient travelling of information at any distance, even into deep space. During my time at university, I’ve had the pleasure of working on some of the very interesting projects with the UTS Rocketry team. Together with the team, I’ve designed and built my first electronics device to safely control the ignition that launches model rockets from a distance. It has been the first time I applied my knowledge into building a real-world application.
I loved the experience and look forward to working on new, exciting and innovative projects.
